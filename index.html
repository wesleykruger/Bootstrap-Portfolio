About Me

I grew up in San Antonio, Texas, and attended college at the University of Texas at Austin. My educational background is in government and business administration. Since graduating, I have worked as a software consultant for government tax agencies in Washington, D.C., and in Santa Fe, New Mexico. I'm thrilled to have finally been able to move back to my home state! In my spare time, I enjoy working out, reading, and listening to podcasts.

While my professional experience is in developing software on the back-end, I am working to also master front-end development. I see a strong future in web development, and have am interested in learning its rapidly-developing technologies. I am currently enrolled in a coding bootcamp at the University of Texas, and am working hard to master these skills.
